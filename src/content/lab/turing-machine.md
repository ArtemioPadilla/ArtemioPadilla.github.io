Simulate Turing machines with an interactive visual tape, head pointer, and state transition table. Step through execution, run at adjustable speed, and explore classic programs including Binary Counter, Palindrome Checker, Busy Beaver (3-state and 4-state), Unary Addition, Binary Addition, and Copy String. Canvas-rendered state diagram shows the automaton structure with highlighted active transitions.
